1. Rise Up

Overview:

“Rise up” is a half-day event dedicated to inspiring and equipping women to reach their full potential, both personally and professionally and for achieving social justice. Through speeches by active fememists,interactive workshops, networking opportunities and valuable insights for women . Empowering women helps to create a more just and equitable society for everyone.

  • Networking Opportunities
